How to Use the App

  1. Step 1: Navigate to the homepage and click "Add a To-Do."
  2. Step 2: Fill out the form with a title and description for your task.
  3. Step 3: Hit the "Submit" button to add the task to your list.
  4. Step 4: View all your tasks in the "Your ToDos" section.
  5. Step 5: Use the "Update" button to edit tasks or "Delete" to remove them.
